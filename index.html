FAQ

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your massage up to 4 hours before the appointment time or reschedule up to 2 hours before the appointment time with no penalty. Canceled & rescheduled appointments with less than thier respective time limits left are subject to a $25 fee.

Does Sama Spa accept insurance?

We currently do not accept insurance as a form of payment. However, you may be able to submit a claim for reimbursement depending on your insurance policy.

Do I need my own massage table?

You do NOT need your own massage table. Your therapists will bring the massage table, oils, fresh sheets, music and all supplies necessary to create your in-home massage experience.

Do I need my own sheets for the massage?

You do NOT need your own sheets, though you may use your own sheets. Your therapists will bring the massage table, oils, fresh sheets, music and all supplies necessary to create your in-home massage experience.

Parking for your massage therapist?

When booking, please give detailed parking and access information in the notes section if needed. Your therapist will do their best to find free convenient parking.

How much clothing should I remove?

Please feel free to undress to your comfort level. Therapists are trained professionals that will always protect your comfort & modesty.

Do I tip my therapist?

All pricing for Sama Spa services are "All-Inclusive", meaning that gratuity(tip) is included. We apologize. Further tips are not accepted.
